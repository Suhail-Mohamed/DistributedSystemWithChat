How to work

  1. Open the lesson HTML file locally in your browser.
  2. Use the answer boxes inside the lesson.
  3. Click Save Draft while working.
  4. Click Download Submission or Copy Submission.
  5. Paste the submission into chat for review.

The answer boxes save to your browser local storage, not to Git. To permanently keep answers, download the submission and commit it under a submissions/ folder or paste it into chat.
